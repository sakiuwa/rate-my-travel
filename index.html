Asakusa

Tokyo, Japan

About the Spot

Asakusa is a popular travel spot, located at the center of Tokyo's shitamachi, where the atmosphere of the old-Tokyo still remains. Asakusa is famous for its long street of souvenir shops. At the beginning of the street is the "Kaminari-mon", which means Thunder Gate. It is decorated with a large red lantern, and this is a very popular picture spot. At the end of the street is the Buddhist temple, Sensoji, and a large pot of smoke. It is said that if you apply the smoke on a certain part of your body, you will see improvement in that area. It is pretty common to see people bathing their head in smoke before big tests.
